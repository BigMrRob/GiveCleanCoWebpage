About Sam

It is privilege to be a part of the Give Clean leadership team. As someone who was born and raised in a family restaurant business, I have always loved anything I can be a part of that is service oriented. My first job in the restaurant was washing dishes and from day one I loved the process of taking something dirty and facilitating the process to clean. 

Our “WHY”— to give, and the “HOW”— through cleaning, is what drew me to Give Clean. I am grateful to work alongside other leaders who have similar passions of positively impacting Memphis through cleaning and engaging with the different communities we have the privilege of working alongside. The community at Give Clean makes the everyday challenges all worth it! 

I can best describe my life outside of work as a “busy blessing.”  I am married to my beautiful wife, Sheley and we are humbled daily by the gift of raising our family together. Our four kids: Virginia Kate, Gracie, Zachary and Reese, keep us on our toes and on our knees! They are the most exhausting joys of our lives. Oh, and I can’t leave out our mini- Bernadoodle, Dabo. We are members of Independent Presbyterian church and love our community there! Occasionally, I find time to play golf but mostly I enjoy spending my free time with family and friends. I love going to concerts, doing anything outdoors, watching my kids play sports, traveling to the beach and the lake, biking with my family, and watching Clemson football.
